1. Introduction

Welcome to Velirc. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Velirc is an offline IRC client â€” this means the app operates entirely on your device and does not send any data to our servers.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any data. Velirc operates completely offline and locally on your device. All your information stays on your device:

  • Connection Settings: IRC server addresses, nicknames, and passwords you configure are stored locally on your device only.
  • Chat History: Any message logs are stored locally on your device.
  • App Preferences: Your settings and preferences are stored locally on your device.

3. No Data Transmission to Us

Velirc does not:

  • Send any data to Vellabs servers
  • Require an account or registration
  • Include analytics or tracking
  • Collect crash reports automatically
  • Display advertisements

4. Third-Party IRC Servers

When you use Velirc to connect to IRC servers, you are communicating directly with those third-party servers. The data you send (messages, nicknames, etc.) is transmitted directly from your device to the IRC server you choose to connect to.

We have no control over and are not responsible for the privacy practices of IRC networks or servers. Please review the privacy policies of the IRC networks you connect to.

5. Local Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. You have complete control over this data:

  • You can delete the app to remove all associated data
  • You can clear app data through your device settings
  • No data is backed up to our servers

6. Children's Privacy

Velirc is not intended for children under 13 years of age. Since we don't collect any data, we cannot knowingly or unknowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
